About BITE ME BUDDY

Our Founder

Sundaram Singh - Founder & Head Chef. A culinary artist dedicated to preserving and sharing authentic traditional flavors through innovative cooking.

Our Brand

BITE ME BUDDY (BMB) represents the perfect blend of tradition and modernity. We bring you the finest authentic flavors in a contemporary setting.

Our Vision

To become the most loved destination for authentic traditional cuisine, creating memorable dining experiences that celebrate our rich culinary heritage.

Our Goal

To serve 1 million happy customers by 2025 while maintaining the highest quality standards and authentic taste that our customers love.

Our Chefs

A team of passionate culinary experts trained in traditional cooking methods, led by Founder Sundaram Singh, bringing generations of recipes to life.

Our Story

Started in 2020 by Sundaram Singh, BMB began with a simple dream - to share the authentic flavors of traditional Indian cuisine with the world.
